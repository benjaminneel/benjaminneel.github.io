About Me

I have a longstanding interest in signaling pathways regulating normal cell proliferation and their derangement in human disease, especially cancer. My research focuses on cellular signaling, with a particular interest in the biology and regulation of protein-tyrosine phosphatases, the role of RAS/ERK pathway mutations in developmental disease and malignancy, and the biology of breast and ovarian cancer.
